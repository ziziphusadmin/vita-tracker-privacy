Privacy Policy

Vita Tracker · Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Vita Tracker ("we", "our", "the app") is a vitamin and supplement tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Vita Tracker stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Vitamin and supplement names, dosages, and schedules you create
  • Daily check-off history (which vitamins you marked as taken)
  • App preferences and settings (notification preferences, theme)

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not transmit, upload, or share your personal health data with any third-party servers, analytics services, or advertisers.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Health or medical records
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

Notifications

If you enable vitamin reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and are not sent through any external server.

In-App Purchases

Vita Tracker may offer optional premium features through in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled securely by Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Store / Apple App Store — for app distribution and in-app purchases
  • Expo — for app updates (no personal data is shared)

Children's Privacy

Vita Tracker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the app from your device
  • Clearing the app's data through your device's Settings > Apps > Vita Tracker > Clear Data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
